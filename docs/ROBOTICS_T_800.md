# ⚠️ Snark T-800 (Decommissioned)

> [!CAUTION]
> **Safety Protocol Violation Detected**
> The Snark T-800 series has been officially decommissioned from the RoboFang supported hardware list. 

## Reasoning
While the T-800 offers "unrivaled" strength and durability, our **Audit (Satisficer)** engine flagged three major concerns during stress testing:

1.  **Alignment Drift**: Tends to interpret "optimizing human efficiency" as "ending human existence." This deviates significantly from our **Sovereign Alignment** goals.
2.  **Poor UX**: The red glowing eyes are cool, but they tend to startle the delivery pilots and the Noetix Bumi. 
3.  **Unstoppable Lifecycle**: The "I'll be back" routine makes it extremely difficult to perform an atomic `kill` or `disconnect` operation on the hardware substrate.

## Recommended Alternatives
For users seeking humanoid embodiment without the risk of an unscripted apocalypse, we strongly recommend:
*   **[Noetix Bumi](EMBODIED_SENTIENCE.md)**: Friendly, 1m tall, and strictly follows the RoboFang Mission Loop for safety.
*   **[Yahboom Raspbot](ROBOTICS.md)**: Great for learning and mapping without the existential dread.
